## Overview

<!-- Replace this with a detailed overview of the concept. -->

My team though of an idea to integrate the fast-growing AI trend into a digital audio workstation, where the AI would help you during the entire process of creating music, suitable for beginners, intermediates and professional artists.

## The Idea

<!-- Describe the idea — what's the vision, what would this enable? -->

We tried to think of a solution to bring in more passionate people who want to create music, but have minimal music theory or notation experience. With our design, we aimed to lower the barrier to music creation.

## Why It's Interesting

Traditional DAWs assume that you have prior experience in the indsutry or that you can already read notation, know theory, etc.
